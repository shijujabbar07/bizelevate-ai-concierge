## The Problem

Every missed call at a dental clinic is a lost appointment. At $150 average value, missing 20 calls a month is $3,000/month — $36,000/year — walking out the door silently.

Most clinics don't track it. None of them have solved it.

---

## What We Do

BizElevate installs AI-powered call handling for clinics. Three capabilities, one platform, no changes to how the clinic operates.

| Capability | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **CustomerReach Respond** | Missed call detected → SMS sent to the patient in under 10 seconds |
| **CustomerReach Answer** | AI voice agent (Casey) answers after-hours calls, takes intake, confirms by SMS |
| **CustomerReach Remind** | Automated appointment reminders at 48h and 2h — reduces no-shows |

All three feed a live management dashboard. Every recovered call, every interaction, visible in one place.
